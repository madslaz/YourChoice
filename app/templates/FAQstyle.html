What are Crisis Pregnancy Centers why should I be wary of them?

Crisis pregnancy centers (also referred to as pregnancy resource centers) intercept women who are considering abortions and attempt to dissuade them from their decision. Centers may intentionally try to misrepresent themselves online, such as implying they offer abortion services or listing inaccurate risks. These centers are usually religiously affiliated and offer services such as pregnancy tests and resources, along with an anti-abortion message. They work to actively disempower women from making one of the most important choices they'll ever make.
