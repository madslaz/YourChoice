Sexual Healthcare IS Healthcare

Your Choice is a safe place to find out more information about abortion legislation and unbiased clinics in the USA.
We believe that this information should be easily accessible to everyone. After a tumultuous 2020, where many US state legislators tried to revoke the already slim options women had, we wanted to create a place that pushes to protect and enhance these rights. The pre-formatted emails on each state page are designed to be sent to senators, allowing for people to advocate for women's health rights in their states with ease.
Sexual healthcare is healthcare, and healthcare is a right.
